How to Play?

  • First Select a category from the List
    OR
    Click "Spin the Wheel" to select a category at Random
  • This will take you to the "Select your Level" page.
    Here you can choose from "Easy", "Medium" or "Hard".
  • You will then be taken to the Quiz, here you will get a total of 10 questions to answer.
  • Choose your answer, this will then be highlighted:
    Green for Correct.
    Red for Wrong.
  • At the End you will be taken to the Results page.
    Here you will be able to see all your answers with the questions, highlighted in the same way in green or red.
  • Yes! you completed the quiz.
    Now click on the "Home" Button to return back to the main home screen.
